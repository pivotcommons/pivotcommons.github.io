Why Pivot Commons?

Great cities emerge at the intersection of people and place.

We are two urban designers driven by a simple belief: better urban experiences create better lives. With four years of individual practice across India, the Middle East, and the UK, we bring a powerful combination of public-sector implementation rigour and international design sophistication.

From mobility exhibitions and heritage walkways to lakefront master plans and smart city precincts, we shape integrated urban environments through multidisciplinary collaboration, stakeholder management, and research-driven design.

What We Do

Services crafted for visionary clients

From concept to tender, we plug in as a skilled, responsive urban design partner for luxury residential estates, commercial developments, and public realm projects.

01

Residential Design

Bespoke residential master planning and estate design that prioritizes human experience, connectivity, and lasting value. From private villas to mixed-use developments, we craft environments where architecture and landscape converge.

02

Urban Planning

Strategic urban design and master planning for cities, corridors, and precincts. We integrate mobility systems, transit-oriented development, public realm strategy, and regulatory compliance into cohesive, implementable visions.

03

Sustainable Architecture

Heritage-sensitive adaptive reuse, climate-resilient public realm design, and sustainable mobility solutions. We balance ecological responsibility with cultural continuity — creating places that endure.
